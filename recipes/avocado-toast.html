Avocado Toast

Description

Looking to eat something fast and healthy for breakfast? Then this is your recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of bread
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 egg
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Peel the avocado and place it in a bowl.
  2. Smash the avocado using a spoon or some other silverware.
  3. Fry the egg.
  4. Put the bread in a toaster.
  5. Place as much avocado as needed on the toast, and then place the egg on top.
  6. Add a sprinkle of salt and pepper on top.
