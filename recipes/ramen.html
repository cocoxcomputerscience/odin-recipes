Ramen

Description

Ramen is a quick and easy meal to fill one's stomach.

Ingredients

  • 1 instant ramen packet
  • 1 stalk of green onions
  • 1 egg
  • 4 pieces of shrimp
  • 1 cup of snow peas

Steps

  1. Boil a small pot of water and then place 1 egg inside.
  2. Wash 1 stalk of green onions and chop into small pieces.
  3. Throw ramen noodles, seasoning and water into a pot.
  4. Once the water starts boiling, toss 4 pieces of shrimp and 1 cup of snow peas inside.
  5. Mix and let the pot simmer until cooked.
  6. Pour the pot into a bowl and toss the green onions on top.
  7. Remove the boiled egg. Peel and cut in half. Then place the egg on top.
